Sunil Gavaskar

I met Sunil Gavaskar in 2019 when he came to Bellevue, Washington. He came to promote the Heart 2 Heart Foundation.

Sunil Gavaskar is a very talented Batsmen. He was a player of the 1970's to 1980's. He was one of the greatest of all time in Batting. Sunil had 2 nicknames: Sunny and Little Master. Sunny scored 10,122 runs in 125 Test Matches! He was the first person to reach the milestone of 10,000 Runs. No one broke that record for a long time, but then Allan Border of Australia scored 10,000 runs on January 2 1993.
